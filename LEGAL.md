## Required Professional Verification

### All outputs from this software MUST be independently verified by qualified professionals before use in construction.

Specifically:

1. **Structural Designs** must be reviewed, certified, and stamped by a Registered Professional Engineer (RPE) licensed to practice in Papua New Guinea.

2. **Building Permits** must be obtained from the PNG Building Board or relevant local authority before any construction begins.

3. **On-Site Inspection** must be conducted by qualified personnel during construction to verify that work conforms to approved designs.

4. **Local Compliance** with all applicable codes, standards, and regulations remains the sole responsibility of the licensed professionals and building owners.
